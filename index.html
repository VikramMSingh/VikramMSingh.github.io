Profile

Hello and welcome to my page! I'm Vikram, born and raised in Mumbai. Moved to Cleveland in 2015 for my graduate degree at Case Western Reserve University. I disagree with Joakim Noah and will say that Cleveland is a really nice city, not as happening as Mumbai but I enjoy it here. I have a Bachelors of Technology in Civil Engineering and a Masters in Engineering and Management. My work experience is quite varied. My first experience was as an Intern Site Engineer for Supreme Infrastructures. Our project was to construct a data center for Vodafone, that is operational during natural or man-made disasters. We had to achieve this goal without minimal environmental impact as the goal was to achieve IGBC (Indian Green Building Council) Gold status.
